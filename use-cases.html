By audience

How different teams use RayX.

EO platforms

Analysis-ready data for your product stack.

You're building analytics or monitoring products on satellite data, and the quality of your input reflectance directly affects everything downstream. SIAC slots into your processing pipeline to provide physics-based atmospheric correction that's consistent across sensors and dates — so your team can focus on the application layer instead of the preprocessing.

Agronomy teams

Crop intelligence that goes beyond NDVI.

You need to advise on crop health, growth stage, and stress — and vegetation indices only take you so far. ARC gives you actual biophysical parameters (leaf area, chlorophyll, canopy structure) that describe what's happening in the crop, not just a colour gradient that approximates it.

Trading & supply

Earlier production signals with traceable reasoning.

You're making positioning or sourcing decisions before official harvest estimates arrive. Yield gives you satellite-derived production direction grounded in crop-state evidence, so you can act earlier and explain the basis of the signal to your risk committee or counterparties.

Insurance & risk

Yield risk assessment backed by a visible audit trail.

Index insurance, portfolio monitoring, and loss adjustment all benefit from an evidence chain you can inspect. When RayX says yield risk is elevated, you can see which crop parameters changed, where, and when — which matters for both underwriting decisions and claims adjudication.
