About

RayX builds earth observation products — not a single vertical.

Our focus is consistency and trust in EO-derived signals. SIAC is a reflectance product designed for multi-sensor harmonisation. ARC and Yield are agriculture product lines built on top of reliable inputs to deliver crop states and yield intelligence.

Product philosophy

  • Consistency first: comparable across sensors and seasons
  • Validation: measurable, auditable calibration and QA
  • Interpretability: connect signals to physical/biological meaning

Product lines

  • SIAC: sensor-invariant surface reflectance
  • ARC: agriculture foundation models
  • Yield: yield intelligence
