RayX — ARC

Agriculture foundation models built on biological and physical understanding of canopy–light interaction. ARC translates EO light signals into crop properties throughout the growing season and improves robustness beyond traditional vegetation indices.

What ARC provides

  • Robust estimation of crop biophysical properties
  • Near real-time crop growth monitoring
  • Stable outputs across sensors and seasons (with SIAC)

Best for

  • Agritech platforms and decision support
  • Monitoring programs
  • Downstream forecasting (including Yield)
Note:
ARC is agriculture-focused and can be used independently.
