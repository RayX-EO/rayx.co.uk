How it works
RayX — SIAC

Sensor-invariant surface reflectance.

Accurate, consistent SR from multispectral → hyperspectral, aligned with CEOS ARD expectations.

  • Radiometric calibration & validation
  • Cross-sensor comparability for time-series
  • QA + traceability
RayX — ARC

Agriculture foundation models.

Physics + biology-guided translation from EO light signals to crop properties through the season.

  • Robust biophysical estimation
  • Near real-time growth monitoring
  • Improves on vegetation indices
RayX — Yield

Yield intelligence.

Field-level yield estimation and 2–3 month ahead yield prediction with factor attribution.

  • 8+ years of yield records (where available)
  • Yield-limiting factor identification
  • Signals for trading & insurance
Positioning

A product suite — not a single vertical.

SIAC is a general EO calibration product for reflectance consistency. ARC and Yield are agriculture products built on top of stable inputs. You can use each component independently.
